    Kosgoda is a small coastal town located in Southern Provincein Sri Lanka. The area is now becoming one of the most vibrant tourist areas in Galle District of Sri Lanka. There are reasons for this, the beautiful beaches and the only place in the country to see Turtles and Turtle hatcheries.Travelling to and from Kosgoda is easy, as the town is located on the A2, also known as Galle Road. All busses and trains heading south to Galle or Matara pass through Kosgoda.
